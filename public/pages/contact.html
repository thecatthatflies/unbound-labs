Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I report a bug?

The best way to report bugs is through GitHub Issues on the relevant project repository. Include steps to reproduce, expected vs actual behavior, and your system information.

Can I contribute to your projects?

Absolutely! All our projects are open source. Check out our GitHub repositories for contribution guidelines and open issues.

Is this software free to use?

Yes, all Unbound Labs projects are free and open source. You can use, modify, and distribute them under their respective licenses.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to emails within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, GitHub Issues often get faster attention from the community.
