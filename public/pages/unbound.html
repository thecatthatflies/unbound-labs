Why unbound?

Built for users who value simplicity and focus.

Distraction-Free

No cluttered toolbars, no unnecessary buttons. Just a clean canvas for browsing the web your way.

No AI Interference

Browse without AI assistants interrupting your flow. You're in control—not an algorithm.

Lightweight & Fast

Stripped of bloat, unbound loads pages quickly and uses minimal system resources.

Privacy Focused

No tracking, no telemetry, no data collection. Your browsing history stays yours.

Open Source

Fully transparent codebase. Review it, modify it, or contribute—it's your browser.

Extensible

Add only what you need. Optional extensions let you customize without compromise.

Technical Details

Built with modern technologies for reliability and performance.

Cross-Platform

Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. One browser everywhere you work.

Security First

Regular security updates and sandboxed browsing protect you from threats.

Standards Compliant

Full support for modern web standards ensures compatibility with all websites.
